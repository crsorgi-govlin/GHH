Registrar
Registrars have been appointed for each local area under Section 7 of the RBD Act, 1969 for registration of births and deaths occurred under their jurisdictional area. Registrars may belong to a municipality, Panchayat, Government health institution or other local authority appointed by the State Government..
Institutions

Public
In case of domiciliary Birth & Death, Citizen needs to inform the occurrence of the event to the respective Registrar, within 21 days of its occurrence. After registration, the certificate needs to be obtained from the Registrar. Where a birth has been registered without name of the child, the same needs to be reported to the Registrar within prescribed period.
